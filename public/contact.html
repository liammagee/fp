Fierce Planet is a collaborative software project jointly developed by RMIT University and University of Western Sydney.

Liam Magee develops the project’s software.

Anthony Richardson is creating much of the content for the related ‘Navigating a Fierce Planet’ project, which uses the technology to create simulations of sustainability scenarios. Nicole Pepperell is project’s Chief Investigator.

Rachel Hendery is also involved in the adaptation of the software to language diffusion, in a project called ‘Geo Language Games’.
